About Me

Frans Prathama is a researcher in the field of data analytics. He is currently working as a Junior Research Engineer at IOChord, Busan, South Korea. In 2021, Frans received his master degree (M.Eng) from Hankuk University of Foreign Studies, Republic of Korea in the field of Data Analytics. Previously, he has been worked as Logistic Controlling specialist at PT. Wings Surya, Indonesia for over than 2 years.
