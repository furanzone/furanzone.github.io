About

Frans Prathama is a Master's student at Data and Business Analytics Lab, which is a part of Industrial and Management Engineering Department, Hankuk University of Foreign Studies, Republic of Korea. His research interest is in human activity recognition, human behavior modeling, and business process management.

Besides his research, he has been active person in various community activities. Previously, he has been worked as Logistic Controlling specialist at Supply Chain Department, PT Wings Surya for over than 2 years.
